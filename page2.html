Pong Project

The goal of this project was to recreate the classic pong game from 1972. In my game their are 2 paddles and 1 ball that the players take turns hitting back and forth. The ball bounces bounces off the top or bottom of the wall and then the paddle. if the player misses then the other player scores the point. if you wish to replay the game after you played it you need to reclick the link Pong Game and it will reset the game.
